ReviewU Webite

Summer 2016, Single-Person Project

Desciption
A review website used to review locations around users.
Details
  • - Website would show locations and their ratings&reviews around users' current positions, these locations are located over a radius of 2 kilometers by default.
  • - Unregistered users could check locations, registered users could post new ratings&reviews for places they have been to.
